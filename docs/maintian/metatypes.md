### Abstract

We describe the structure of bones types - metatypes.

Meta (from the Greek μετά, meta, meaning "after" or "beyond") is a prefix meaning "more comprehensive" or "transcending".

We have all sorts of words we could use, for example class, kind, sort, category, set, group, flavour, family, and 
so on, but our roots are in Smalltalk hence we use the term meta.


### Overview

There are 6 meta types.

Atom

Union

Exponentials
lists

functions

Schema Variables - basically an atom used as an identifier in type schemas
